Enable USB Debugging

Settings → About → tap Build Number 7×, then Developer Options → USB Debugging

2

Connect Your Device

Plug in via USB or use "Add Wireless" to connect over WiFi (same network)

3

Mirror & Control

Click a device card → Open Mirror. Use mouse & keyboard to control your phone
